Our Goal

Our project goal is to deliver a feasible, intuitive, and easy-to-use product to the client. We will put in our best effort to meet all the client's needs, as well as provide any technical input for improvement when given the opportunity. A successful product will allow the client to automate synchronization of planet catalogues with his own with the ability to verify and accept/deny discrepencies between catalogues. There will also be focus on localizing the product to allow offline accessibility and making the product customizable in case the client wants to expand the scope of the catalogues. As more requirements come up, we will stick to our goal of having an efficient and user-friendly application.
